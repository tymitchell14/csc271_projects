RateGames


RateGames is an all in one place where you can rate and write reviews for video games. You can have all of your reviews here no matter what platform you played it on. Look at reviews of others to see their opinions or get reccomendations for your backlog. Create lists of your favorite games to let others know what you enjoy. RateGames is the best platform to review and catalog the games you love.


Features

  • Create reviews for games where you can rate them from 1-5 and write your thoughts.
  • Create lists of your favorite games or games that you think belong together
  • View reviews of others and like on the ones you enjoyed.
  • View lists of others to get ideas on what games to add to your backlog
  • Look at your profile to see the catalog of games you've played and reviewed.
