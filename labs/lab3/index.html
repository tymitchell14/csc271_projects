My Favorite Dish: Tacos

Tacos are my favorite dish because there are a lot of options for what different ingredients you can put in them For the meat you can do ground beef, chicken, or steak. You can also put many different options for vegetables and toppings. One of my favorite things about tacos is that you can spice them up with hot sauce if you are feeling up to it. Some special memories I have about tacos is me and my grandma used to go to Taco Bell often when I was a teenager.
